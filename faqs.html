How big is a full-grown Pomsky?

Generally, the key factor that determines the size of a full-grown dog is the size and genetics of its parents, the Pomeranian and Siberian Husky. The average weight is between 20-30 pounds. On the other hand, the average height is between 10-15 inches.

How big is a teacup Chihuahua?

There is no standardized measurement for a Teacup Chihuahua dog, but in general terms, the height of the dog is between 6 to 9 inches (15 to 23 cm). Still, some Chihuahuas are 12 to 15 inches (30 to 38 cm) tall though it is debatable if they could be considered as Teacup or Toy Chihuahuas.
