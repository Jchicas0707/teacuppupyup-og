Tea Cup Pomeranian

More about the Breed

Teacup Pomeranian is a small, active and adorable dog breed. Like any other type of breeds, the Teacup Pomeranian not only has a long and interesting history behind it but also several positives and negatives. On the positive side, this breed tends to have a pretty sweet temperament.

Tea Cup Chihuahua

More about the Breed

The tiny teacup Chihuahua is a truly small dog. As an adult, could weigh around 5lbs, or even less. Unfortunately, this miniature size causes some serious health issues. Standard sized Chihuahuas usually have long lifespans, but the health issues in teacup Chihuahuas can mean they live much shorter lives.

Pomsky

More about the Breed

A Pomsky typically has a soft, fluffy, silky coat, prick ears and a furry tail that swishes over the back. The Pomsky is usually bred from a Siberian Husky female and a Pomeranian male. Breedings are usually done through artificial insemination because of the size difference in the two breeds.
