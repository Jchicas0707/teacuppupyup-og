Great Variety of Teas and Of Course Tea Cup Pups

We pay handsomely to serve you heirloom leaves – a superior product that has been overshaddowed by younger, less distinctive, higher-yielding varietals (heirlooms now comprise less than 3% of Japan’s annual harvest). Imported directly from our partner farm in Kyoto, theese leaves are untouched by chemicals, handpicked, and preserved in the traditional Japanese fashion. The result is incredibly nuanced, complex elixirs without a hint of bitterness.

We provide high-quality care to our teacup puppies and dogs in order to socialize and find them homes at each pup's own pace. Whenever possible, we go outside of the area to search for the ideal teacup puppies!
